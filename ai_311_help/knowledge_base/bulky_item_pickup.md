# Bulky Item Pickup

Residents can schedule free pickup of large items that don't fit in regular trash bins. This includes furniture, mattresses, appliances, large electronics, and other oversized household items.

Single-family homes and small multi-unit buildings (under 5 units) are entitled to up to 10 free bulky item pickups per year through Recology. Larger apartment buildings have different arrangements — check with your property manager.

To schedule a pickup, call Recology at 415-330-1300 or use their online booking system. You'll need to describe the items, give the count, and provide your address. Pickups are typically scheduled within 2 weeks of the request.

On pickup day, items must be placed at the curb by 6am, in the same location as your regular trash bins. Items should be set out separately from regular trash, ideally the night before to avoid blocking the sidewalk for long periods.

Accepted items: couches, chairs, tables, mattresses, box springs, dressers, refrigerators, stoves, washers, dryers, TVs, computers, monitors, microwaves.

Not accepted: hazardous materials (paint, batteries, chemicals), construction debris, automobile parts, items larger than 6 feet in any dimension. For these, contact Recology about special arrangements or use the Tunnel Avenue facility.

Illegal dumping of large items on the street, in alleys, or in vacant lots can result in fines of $500-$1,500. If you witness illegal dumping, report it through 311 with a description of the vehicle or person if safe to do so.
