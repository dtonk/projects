# Muni Lost & Found and Transit Issues

San Francisco Municipal Railway (Muni) is operated by SFMTA and serves the city with buses, light rail (Metro), historic streetcars, and cable cars.

**Lost items on Muni:**
Items left on Muni vehicles are turned in to the Muni Lost & Found at 1 South Van Ness Ave. To inquire about lost property:
- Call 311 and ask to be connected to Muni Lost & Found
- Visit in person Monday–Friday, 8am–5pm
- Items are typically held for 30 days before being donated or disposed of
- High-value items (phones, wallets, keys) are held longer

**Muni service complaints** (driver behavior, route issues, delays, accessibility failures):
- File a complaint through 511 (SF Bay transit info line) or the SFMTA website
- Complaints about unsafe driving or ADA violations are taken seriously and may result in formal investigation
- You can also report issues directly through the Muni feedback form at sfmta.com

**Reporting Muni fare evasion or safety concerns** on vehicles: call 311 or press the passenger emergency strip inside the vehicle to alert the operator.

**Cable cars:** Cable car lost items go to a separate office at the Cable Car barn (1201 Mason St). Call 415-701-2311.

**Clipper card issues:** Clipper card problems (charges, balance disputes, lost cards) are handled by the Clipper customer service center, not Muni directly. Call 877-878-8883 or visit clippercard.com.

Muni Metro elevator and escalator outages are tracked in real time on the SFMTA website and 511.org. Report outages at the station booth or through 311.
