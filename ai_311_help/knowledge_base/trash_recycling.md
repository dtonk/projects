# Trash and Recycling Pickup

Residential trash, recycling, and compost collection is provided by Recology under contract with the city. Pickup schedules vary by neighborhood — most addresses get weekly pickup on a specific weekday.

To check your pickup schedule, look up your address on the Recology website or call 311. Each property typically receives three bins: black (landfill), blue (recycling), and green (compost/organics).

Bins should be placed at the curb by 6am on your collection day and removed within 24 hours after pickup. Leaving bins on the sidewalk for extended periods can result in a warning followed by a fine.

If your bin is missed on collection day, report it through 311 within 48 hours. Recology will schedule a return pickup, usually within 1-2 business days.

To request additional bins, replace damaged bins, or change bin sizes, contact Recology directly at 415-330-1300. Bin replacement for damaged bins is free; size upgrades may affect your service rate.

Hazardous waste (paint, batteries, electronics, motor oil, household chemicals) cannot go in any bin. Schedule a free household hazardous waste pickup through Recology, or drop items off at the Tunnel Avenue Resource Recovery Facility.

Large items like furniture and appliances are handled through the bulky item pickup program (see separate FAQ).
