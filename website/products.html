Products

Simple tools built well

When we see a gap in the market, we build to fill it. Our consulting work surfaces the same problems over and over — sometimes the right answer is a product, not another engagement.

First Product

Low Tech Maps

Beta

We saw a gap in affordable, easy-to-use mapping tools. Existing options either required a GIS specialist, an engineering team, or a budget most venues don't have.

Low Tech Maps is our answer: an interactive map in minutes. Start with your own PDF or a street map, add clickable points of interest, and share a mobile-ready map with a live GPS "you are here" dot. No GIS skills, no developer, no app to download — built for zoos, festivals, parks, and any outdoor space where people need to find their way around.
