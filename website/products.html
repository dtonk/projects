How It Works

Three steps, no manual

Designed for the marketing coordinator, the parks department staffer, the festival volunteer — not the GIS consultant.

Upload your PDF

Your existing venue map — the one you already have, probably as a PDF. We convert it to an image automatically.

Orient & align

Rotate the satellite view to match your map's orientation, then drag the corner handles to line things up. The opacity slider helps you check your work.

Publish & share

Click "Looks Good." Your georeferenced map is live at a clean URL. Visitors get GPS positioning, mobile support, and no app to install.

Who It's For

Any outdoor venue with a PDF map and visitors who get lost

We built Low Tech Maps for zoos, botanical gardens, state and county fairs, music festivals, university open days, and large parks — anywhere that has an existing printed map and visitors who need to navigate a real space.

The operator publishes once. Visitors open a URL, grant GPS access, and see exactly where they are on the actual venue map — offline capable after first load.
