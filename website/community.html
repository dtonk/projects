Community

Discounted rates for work that matters

We believe good data infrastructure shouldn't only be accessible to companies with big budgets. If your organization fits one of the categories below, we offer significantly reduced rates.

Who Qualifies

Organizations we want to support

The following types of organizations are eligible for our community rates. If you're not sure whether you qualify, reach out — we're happy to talk.

01

Nonprofits

501(c)(3) organizations and their equivalents. You're doing important work with limited resources — your data systems should still be solid.

02

Employee-Owned Businesses

Co-ops, ESOPs, and other employee-owned structures. We believe in the model and want to help it succeed with better data foundations.

03

Open Data Initiatives

Projects focused on making public data more accessible, usable, or transparent. If your work makes data available to everyone, we want to support it.

04

People Doing Good

You don't need a 501(c)(3) to be doing something worth supporting. If you're working on something that helps people and need a hand with your data, reach out. We're happy to help.

How It Works

Same services, lower cost

Community rate engagements get the same quality of work as any other project. We don't cut corners — we cut the price. The scope, deliverables, and level of attention are identical.

The discount applies to all of our consulting services: assessments, architecture, governance, analytics infrastructure, and training. We'll work with you to find a scope and pace that fits your budget.

No formal application process. Just mention it when you reach out and we'll take it from there.

Get Started

Let's talk about your project

Reach out and let us know what you're working on. Mention that you're interested in community rates and we'll go from there.
