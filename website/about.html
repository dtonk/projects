About

Lower Technology LLC

A consulting and software company built on the conviction that most technology is unnecessarily complicated — and that anyone who needs good data deserves better options than what's currently out there.

The Name

Through the pass, not over the mountain

The logo is a mountain pass — the route through the valley instead of over the summit. Less impressive to talk about at a conference. Much better to actually travel.

This is the whole idea. Enterprise data platforms are built to scale to hundreds of engineers and billions of events. Most of our clients have one analyst and a spreadsheet. The right answer for them isn't a smaller version of the enterprise platform — it's a different mountain range entirely.

The name is a little tongue-in-cheek, sure. But it's also a genuine position. Lower technology — simpler, more direct, built for humans who have other jobs — is usually the better technology.

How We Work

A few things we actually believe

Simplicity

If it needs a diagram to explain, it needs to be simpler

We bias toward the boring solution. The one your team can operate at 2am when something breaks. Complex systems are impressive until they're not your problem anymore.

Ownership

You should be able to run it without us

Every engagement ends with documented, maintainable infrastructure your team can own. We're not interested in creating dependency. Good consulting should reduce the need for more consulting.

Fit

The right tool, not the impressive tool

We'll tell you if DuckDB is a better answer than Snowflake for your use case. We'll recommend Metabase over Looker if it fits your budget. The goal is solving your problem, not building a portfolio piece.

Mission

Good data shouldn't require an engineering team

A lot of organizations are chronically underserved by software — they get enterprise tools that assume a level of staffing they'll never have. We exist to close that gap, at least a little.
